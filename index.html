Full job description

At The DanFX Trading Academy, we are dedicated to providing exceptional service in the dynamic world of cryptocurrency and financial markets. Our mission is to empower individuals by equipping them with the knowledge and tools they need to succeed in trading. We focus on delivering cutting-edge educational resources that help both novice and seasoned traders navigate the complexities of cryptocurrency, trading strategies, and market analysis.
Overview of the Role
We are looking for a skilled and attentive Payments Processing Specialist to manage payment transactions for our trading courses, membership subscriptions, and client services. This vital role ensures accuracy, compliance, and seamless customer experiences. As part of our dynamic and growing team, youll not only play a key part in the day-to-day operations but also gain valuable exposure to the trading and financial education industry.
The ideal candidate will be agile, attentive to detail, and eager to contribute to the success of our team.
Salary:
$54,750 per year.
Flexible Working Hours:
We understand the importance of work-life balance. This role offers flexible scheduling to accommodate your needs.
How You Will Make Contributions That Matter:
Payment Processing Duties:
  • Assist with processing user payments and ensuring accurate transaction records.
  • Respond to user inquiries related to payment issues with professionalism and courtesy.
  • Collaborate with the team to enhance user experience and streamline payment processes.
  • Sort and distribute incoming & outgoing payments.
  • Perform all other related tasks.
Qualifications:
  • No previous experience or formal education required.
  • Excellent communication skills, both verbal and written.
  • Ability to manage multiple tasks efficiently and with attention to detail.
  • Positive attitude and willingness to learn.
  • Good organizational skills and the ability to work independently in a remote environment.
What We Offer:
  • Competitive salary of $54,750 per year.
  • Comprehensive group insurance benefits.
  • Group retirement pension plan with employer contributions.
  • Opportunities to purchase company stocks.
  • Employee assistance program for you and your family.
At The Dan FX Trading Academy, we are dedicated to fostering a diverse and inclusive workplace and welcome applications from individuals of all backgrounds. We believe that your unique contributions will help us achieve our mission in the digital asset industry.
